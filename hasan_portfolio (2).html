I'm Hasan Abdullah, a UI/UX and Graphic Designer who believes design should be both beautiful and functional. I work at the intersection of visual craft and user psychology — creating interfaces that feel intuitive and look exceptional.

My toolkit spans Figma for interface design and prototyping, Adobe Photoshop for image manipulation and compositing, and Adobe Illustrator for vector-based brand work. Every project I take on is treated as a complete visual system.
